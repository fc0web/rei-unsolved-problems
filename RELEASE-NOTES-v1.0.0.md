## Honest framing (Paper 83 principle)

- No conjecture is claimed "solved".
- "Closed" means: Lean 4 formal proof with zero sorry against Mathlib v4.27.0.
- "Empirically resolved" means: verified numerically at stated scale.
- All results acknowledge their honest gap.
